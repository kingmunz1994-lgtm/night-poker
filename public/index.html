How the ZK works

🔀
Mental Shuffle
Each player XOR-encrypts the deck with their secret key. No one — including you — can see the final card order.
🔐
Private Deal
Your hole cards are encrypted with all other players' keys. Only you can decrypt them. Opponents get a commitment hash.
⚡
Off-Chain Betting
Betting rounds run in a state channel — instant, no gas per action. Only showdown and settlement touch the chain.
🏆
ZK Showdown
At showdown each player proves their cards match the on-chain commitment. Cheating is cryptographically impossible.
